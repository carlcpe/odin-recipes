Pancit Canton Recipe

Recpie from Panlasang Pinoy

Description

Pancit Canton is a vibrant and flavorful Filipino noodle dish that is a staple at celebrations and family gatherings. Made with flour noodles, this stir-fried delight features a medley of meats such as chicken, shrimp, and pork, combined with an assortment of vegetables like cabbage, carrots, and bell peppers. The noodles are cooked in a savory sauce made from soy sauce, oyster sauce, and chicken broth, absorbing the rich flavors and creating a harmonious blend of taste and texture. The dish is often garnished with calamansi or lemon wedges, adding a refreshing citrus kick.

The beauty of Pancit Canton lies in its versatility and the balance of its ingredients. Each component, from the tender meats to the crisp vegetables, contributes to a satisfying and hearty meal. This dish not only showcases the Filipino love for noodles but also highlights the cultural tradition of communal feasting, as it is commonly served during special occasions and festive events. Pancit Canton exemplifies the Filipino culinary ethos of creating flavorful, nourishing, and shareable dishes that bring people together.

Ingredients

  • 250 grams flour stick noodles
  • 4 ounces pork, thinly sliced
  • 1 piece Chinese sausage, sliced
  • 1 piece onion, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon garlic, minced
  • 8 to 10 pieces shrimp, shell removed
  • 10 to 12 pieces snap peas
  • 3/4 cup carrot, julienned
  • 1 piece cabbage, small, chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup flat leaf parsley, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Place 2 cups of ice and 3 cups water in a large bowl. Set aside.
  2. Boil 6 cups of water in a cooking pot.
  3. Once the water starts to boil, blanch the snap peas, carrots, and cabbage for 35 to 50 seconds. Quickly remove the vegetables and immerse in bowl with ice cold water. Drain the water after 2 minutes and set aside.
  4. Heat a large wok or cooking pot and pour-in the cooking oil.
  5. Saut√© the onion and garlic.
  6. Add the pork and sausage slices and continue to cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Add-in soy sauce and oyster sauce. Stir.
  8. Pour-in chicken broth and water. Add salt and pepper. Let boil and continue to cook for 5 to 10 minutes.
  9. Put-in the shrimp and parsley. Cook for 3 minutes. Add more water if needed.
  10. Put-in the flour noodles. Gently toss until the noodles absorb the liquid.
  11. Add-in the blanched vegetables. Toss and cook for 1 to 2 minutes.
  12. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve.
  13. Share and enjoy!
